# Product Brief

## Product

**GastroLedger** is a multi-tenant operational SaaS for restaurant chains and
franchises. It centralizes menu engineering, purchasing, lot-aware inventory,
production, store operations and profitability analysis.

## Problem

Restaurant groups frequently know sales totals but cannot explain the real
consumption, waste and cost behind them. Recipes, purchase units, production yields,
transfers and physical counts live in disconnected spreadsheets. The result is weak
cost control, late expiry detection and low trust in theoretical margins.

## Value Proposition

GastroLedger turns recipes into an operational inventory ledger:

- purchase units convert into precise consumption units;
- production converts ingredients into traceable prepared lots;
- imported or simulated sales consume recipe components;
- FEFO allocation, waste and counts explain inventory movement;
- theoretical and actual costs remain comparable by branch and period.

## Target Users

- restaurant holding administrator;
- branch manager;
- chef or production lead;
- warehouse operator;
- purchasing manager;
- workforce coordinator;
- franchise controller;
- cost and profitability analyst.

## First-Version Boundaries

GastroLedger is not:

- a POS or payment processor;
- an accounting, invoicing or payroll system;
- a paid-subscription billing platform;
- a marketplace or supplier integration hub;
- dependent on email, identity, tax, bank or messaging APIs.

## Success Outcomes

- Every accepted inventory change is explained by an immutable ledger entry.
- Recipe cost changes can be traced to ingredient cost or yield changes.
- Sales imports produce deterministic consumption or actionable shortage exceptions.
- Operators identify expiring lots and variances before they become unexplained loss.
- A holding can compare branches without mixing tenant data.
