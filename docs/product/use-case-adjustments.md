# Adjustments To Proposed Use Cases

## Added Or Consolidated

- Added company registration as the SaaS onboarding entry point.
- Combined branch and warehouse configuration.
- Combined recipe costing, contribution margin and suggested price.
- Combined blind physical count and reconciliation into one controlled lifecycle.
- Combined weekly scheduling and attendance into workforce operations.
- Combined cost variance and menu engineering into one analytics workspace.

## Modified

- Expiry allocation uses FEFO instead of PEPS when expiration dates exist.
- Automatic purchase orders become purchase suggestions requiring approval.
- Supplier credit notes become expected adjustment records.
- Payroll becomes a worked-hours and absence report.
- Royalty liquidation becomes a non-financial estimate.
- Franchise debt blocking becomes a manually governed ordering hold.
- Sales and cash data are operational records, not real payment transactions.

## Removed From V1

- Paid subscription plans and billing.
- Real payments, settlements, collections and money transfers.
- Accounting entries, tax documents and formal payroll.
- External POS, identity, messaging, banking or supplier APIs.
